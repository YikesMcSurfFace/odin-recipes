Wacky Lasagna Recipe

Tasty ass lasagna you and your whole fam can enjoy!

Just like the lasagna your mom used to make, double the cheese and double the meat!

Ingredients

  • 4 Tomatoes
  • 1lb celery
  • Dragon Egg
  • Goat Milk
  • Goat Cheese
  • Orc Meat

Steps:

  1. Go to the mountains of Mordor and fetch an egg
  2. Raise the little dragon as a sous-chef
  3. Train the little guy to make lasagna
  4. Profit! Enjoy that Wacky Lasagna!
