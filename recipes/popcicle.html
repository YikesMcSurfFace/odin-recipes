Paint Popcicle Recipe

Paint & Popcicle

Just lick it for the paint! hard on the paint!

Ingredients

  • 1 Bucket of Paint
  • 3 Dragon fruits
  • 1 Bucket of Ice

Steps:

  1. Dump paint into ice bucket
  2. Cut up dragon fruit and throw them in too
  3. Put bucket in frezer and stick a pole in the middle
  4. Now you got a big ass popcicle!
