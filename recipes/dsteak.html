Dragon Steak Recipe

Dragon Steak cooked at home!

Just juicy and delicabile steak

Ingredients

  • 1 Kilo Dragon Meat
  • 1 tbs of Assorted Spice
  • 1 tbs salt
  • 2 tbs BBQ Sauce

Steps:

  1. Find hot dragons in your local area
  2. Trick him and throw him in your cauldron
  3. Add spices
  4. Take out dragon, lather with BBQ sauce and enjoy!
